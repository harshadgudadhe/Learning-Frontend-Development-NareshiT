Structural Elements for Content Flow

These tags help organize content and control basic document flow.

Horizontal Rule

The hr tag creates a thematic break in content, often rendered as a horizontal line.

Above this line is one section of content.

Line Break

The br tag creates a line break, forcing the text to the next line without starting a new paragraph.
